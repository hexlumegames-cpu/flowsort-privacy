📋 Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Hexlume Games handles personal data in the mobile application Flow Sort: Color Tube Puzzle (app ID: com.hexlumegames.flowsort).

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Flow Sort does not directly collect any personal information. However, in-app advertising and analytics services may involve third-party data processing.

👤 Information We Collect

The app does not directly collect or transmit the following to our servers:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Precise location data
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Contacts or calendar information

Data stored locally on your device includes only:

  • Game progress (completed levels, score)
  • App settings (sound, music preferences)
  • Ad consent preference (UMP/GDPR)

📣 Advertising – Google AdMob

The app uses Google AdMob (Google LLC) to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect the following to measure ad performance, deliver personalized ads, and prevent fraud:

  • Advertising identifier (GAID / Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage statistics and interaction data
  • Device information (model, OS version)

Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Manage your ad preferences: adssettings.google.com

🌍 GDPR & User Consent

Users located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland will be presented with a consent prompt for ad personalization via Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) upon first launch.

  • You may withdraw your consent at any time.
  • If you decline personalization, contextual ads may still be shown.
  • Your consent preference is stored locally on your device only.

🔒 Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. Third-party providers (Google AdMob) maintain their own security standards. The app does not allow mixed content (HTTP) connections; only HTTPS is used.

👶 Children's Privacy

Flow Sort is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from this age group. If you believe your child has used the app, please contact us.

The app is published in the "Everyone" category on Google Play. Ad content does not include child-directed personalized advertising.

📜 Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access and correct data held about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to data processing
  • Withdraw your ad consent

To submit a request: hexlume.games@gmail.com

To delete all game data, simply uninstall the application from your device.

🔗 Third-Party Links

The app may contain links to other Hexlume Games titles. These links direct to Google Play Store pages; Google's privacy terms apply.

✏️ Policy Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. We will publish any significant changes on this page. Continued use of the application constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
